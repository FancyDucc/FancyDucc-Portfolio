Duality
A fanmade Forsaken character

Duality writing, abilities, and the overall idea

This character I made in a day or so was the result of a dream after playing a tad bit too much of Forsaken.

After having the dream, I wrote quite a bit of the character, abilities, just some fanmade little ideas.

Below is what I wrote:

Killer: Duality

Appearance: black and white limbs, split down the middle in color, it has a tie and a hat on their person and their head is also split into two colors

Duality has two signature swords that he keeps at his waists when not in use at all times, one white, one black, these swords are Duality’s soul, as if there’s anymore of that anyway, if either of these swords are destroyed, Duality may not exist to see it be that way.

Duality is a being that once was a player, though after years of torment, disregard, and abuse, his death released all of the pent up anger and resentment he had to the citizens of Roblox in the form of an entity.

Before being known as the feared entity Duality, he was known as David, someone who kept quiet and was commonly made fun of, shamed, disregarded, and ignored, which be the case of Dualities violent tendencies.

Stats:

Cost - 1850

Difficulty - 5

Damage - 95

Health - 925

Regular Speed - 8.25

Sprinting Speed - 28

Stamina - 110

Stamina Loss Per Second - 10

Stamina Gain Per Second - 20

Duality can build up massive damage at the cost of time, meaning charging up a powerful attack is slow but impactful.
