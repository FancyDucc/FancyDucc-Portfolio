Payment Information

Understanding that everyone is unique, and every project comes with its own set of requirements, I try to provide flexibility when it comes to payment methods. Whether you are an independent creator on a budget or a well-established organization, rest assured that I've got a payment method that suits you. You can pay for my services through a variety of methods, including Robux, group funds on Roblox, USD, or Cashapp. My goal is to make the payment process as seamless and convenient as possible for you, so you can focus more on the creative aspect of your game and less on the logistics.

I do not provide a list of payments on this page as everyone is different and I am willing to adjust how much I receive based off of the success of the game, how much I do, and anything you may request of me, you pay what you think I deserve, no questions asked.

Currently, I am very generous with helping others and their games, so if I come to you in ANY social media (Discord, Instagram, Roblox, email, etc.) then payments are out of the picture as I am solely asking to be a part of game development for learning, interest, helping out, and of course, fun!

The list of games I am currently offering free services for are as follows:

  • The Skinwalker (By Otrix and Zaku)
  • BIO-HAZARD (By The Conglomerate of Anomalous Activity/Ark Hannold)

Apart from unable to pay fully, you can also allow me special permissions to the game if/when the game gets published. (In-game tag above head, commands, discounted/free gamepass/es, etc)
